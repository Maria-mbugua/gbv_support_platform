Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive support and resources to survivors of Gender-Based Violence. We are dedicated to creating a safe space for healing, empowerment, and advocacy.

Support Services

  • Crisis Hotline: Reach out to our 24/7 hotline for immediate assistance.
  • Counseling Services: Access confidential counseling and mental health support.
  • Legal Assistance: Get legal advice and support for GBV cases.
  • Safe Shelters: Find safe shelters and emergency housing options.

Resource Directory

  • National GBV Hotline - Available 24/7 to provide immediate support and resources.
  • Legal Aid Kenya - Offers legal assistance and advice for GBV cases.
  • Women’s Rights Organizations - Connect with organizations dedicated to protecting women's rights.
  • Mental Health Services - Access mental health support and counseling services.
  • Safe Housing Solutions - Find safe housing options for survivors in need of emergency shelter.
